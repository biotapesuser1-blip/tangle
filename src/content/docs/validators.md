## Validators

Validators are a crucial part of the Heroglyph Protocol ecosystem. Their role includes producing blocks and ensuring the network operates securely.

### Identity for Validators
Currently, the Identity module is limited to validators. However, once the delegation system is fully deployed, it will be accessible to all participants.

### Use of Ticker by Validators
Validators can utilize tickers to:

- Create concise identifiers
- Ensure configurations are consistently optimized

Advanced validators may find tickers particularly advantageous for improving network interactions.
