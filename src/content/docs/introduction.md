## Introduction

Heroglyph Protocol utilizes graffiti from a produced block to execute custom logic developed by anyone in the ecosystem. Our mission is to promote decentralization within the chain and to empower our heroes, the validators.

The protocol operates through two core modules:

### Identity
Similar to Ethereum Name Service (ENS), the Identity module allows users to create an identity and link it to a wallet. This is essential to execute blocks, as Heroglyph Protocol will not process a block without one.

### Ticker
The Ticker module is intended for advanced validators and developers to integrate their smart contracts. Utilizing a Harberger tax and hijacking system, tickers can be bought or lost due to insufficient funds in the tax system.
