## Ticker

The Ticker module is tailored for advanced validators and developers to register their protocols with Heroglyph Protocol.

### Features of Ticker
- **Ownership:** Tickers integrate a Harberger tax and hijacking mechanism, allowing others to acquire or lose them based on tax compliance.
- **Purpose:** Developers use tickers to connect their protocols, while validators may utilize them for shorter and optimized names.

Ensure adequate funds are available to maintain ownership of your ticker.
